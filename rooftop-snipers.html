Rooftop Snipers is a hilarious physics-based shooting game that pits two players against each other in rooftop combat. Players must master the art of shooting and dodging while dealing with amusing ragdoll physics that make every match unpredictable and entertaining.

The game features:

  • Local 2-player multiplayer mode
  • Single-player vs AI option
  • Physics-based gameplay
  • Various character options
  • Multiple rooftop environments
  • Simple but challenging controls
  • Ragdoll mechanics
  • Quick rounds format
