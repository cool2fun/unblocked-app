Hanger unblocked

Hanger is a thrilling physics-based swinging game where you play as a ragdoll character using a grappling rope to swing through challenging levels. Test your timing and precision as you navigate through hazardous obstacles and reach the finish line.

Core Features

  • Swing through levels using a grappling rope
  • Navigate past dangerous obstacles
  • Time your swings perfectly
  • Collect coins along the way
  • Ragdoll physics mechanics

How To Play

Time your rope swings carefully to maintain momentum. Watch out for obstacles and plan your path ahead. Use the momentum of your swings to reach distant platforms.
