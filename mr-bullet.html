Mr Bullet Unblocked

Mr Bullet is an engaging physics-based puzzle shooter where players must use strategic shooting and ricochets to eliminate targets with limited bullets. Every level presents a new challenge requiring creative thinking and precise aiming to complete missions efficiently.

The game features:

  • Physics-based shooting mechanics
  • Multiple challenging levels
  • Strategic puzzle elements
  • Limited bullet gameplay
  • Various mission types
  • Achievement system
  • Score-based rewards
  • Progressive difficulty
